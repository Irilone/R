Dear Robin,

Happy 66th. After miscalculating time, as per usual, I had to pull an all-nighter on this present. I apologize for the tardiness.

You have a rare sense for time and rhythm. You also know how easy it is to get lost in the work. In the details. In the moment. Over time, your balance and rhythm might rub off on me, if I should be so lucky.

Thank you for welcoming Diko and me into your life with an open heart. It means more than I can say. As we say in Sweden, du sätter guldkant på tillvaron. The cherry on top in American. Your presence makes life feel more precious, just by being there.

Thank you for being part of our life.

We love you very much and look forward to what is ahead. The four of us. Together.

Eternally yours, with all our love,

Dikoros & Dorian
